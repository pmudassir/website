Nandi Hills

  • Place: Bangalore
  • Date: 18 Aug, 2019

The Nandi Hills is an amazing place for visiting weekend getaway from Bangalore that lures many to plan a trip from Bangalore to Nandi Hills. I would surely recommend a bike ride through the hills in the early morning to get a view of an awesome sunrise. It has now become a well-known holiday spot The place, also called Nandidurga, has mesmerizing beauty, and watching the sunrise is an amazing experience. It is also ideal for bird watching. Watching the surrounding landscape and enjoying the cool breeze is the perfect relaxation recipe for your mind and body. There are some important historic ruins including temples and forts that are thronged by thousands of visitors.you can indulge in cycling and adventure sports like Paragliding, scenic beauty can best be enjoyed by walking.
