PUNE

Pune boasts several historical monuments and museums. There is the Aga Khan Palace, Pataleshwar, the Chaturshringi Temple, the Sinhagad Fort, and the famous Shaniwar Wada are some of the historical and architectural gems worth visiting. Several neighboring places can be accessed from Pune. Hill stations like Mahabaleshwar, Panchgani, Lonavala, and Khandala are close by, and so is Mumbai. Another major draw for people visiting Pune is Goa. No matter when you visit, Goa can be enjoyed all the time, with its beaches and happening party scene. Pune is shopping heaven. From budget shoppers to luxury lovers, there are many options for everyone. There are malls selling international brands as well as famous street shopping venues where you get a good bargain. Some important shopping destinations are Koregaon Park, MG Road, FC Road, and JM Road. Phoenix Market City, Amanora Town Mall, Seasons Mall, and West End Mall are the major high-end shopping destinations of Pune. The people of Pune are simple, sincere, and liberal. Since Pune is a busy city, the locals usually remain engrossed in their work but are still friendly and helpful. Pune is a liberal city where you can wear anything you want without being judged or feeling awkward.
